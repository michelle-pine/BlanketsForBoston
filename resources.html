Resources

Charities

If you want more information about the places we donate to, here are their websites:

  • Project Linus  - a nonprofit which donates blankets to children in need, particularly those in hospitals. There are chapters of this organization in various parts of Massachusetts.
  • Warm Up America!  - an organization which gives knitted blankets and hats to hospitals, shelters, and homeless people, as well as victims of disaster.
  • My Very Own Blanket  - a charity which donates blankets and pillows to children in foster care.
  • Blanket Hearts  - a charity which specializes in creating no-sew blankets for homeless people, people in hospitals, and veterans.
