Donate

Generous people help create our blankets – and warmth throughout Boston! Donations keep the warmth spreading.

We need fabric to host our Blanket Building Events, so we’d love if you could drop some off for us. We prefer 60 inch by 60 inch pieces of fleece fabric, in colors of your choice! Fleece usually costs around $6.00 per yard at local fabric stores (with some variation). We also accept yarn and wool for crocheting, scissors, and donations of food for events.

Donate fabrics 

If you can’t find fabrics, that’s okay too! A small monetary donation can get us lots of fabric for blankets.

Proposed Charities

These are the charities that we donate our blankets to:

  • Project Linus - a nonprofit which donates blankets to children in need, particularly those in hospitals. There are chapters of this organization in various parts of Massachusetts.
  • Warm Up America! - an organization which gives knitted blankets and hats to hospitals, shelters, and homeless people, as well as victims of disaster.
  • My Very Own Blanket - a charity which donates blankets and pillows to children in foster care.
  • Blanket Hearts - a charity which specializes in creating no-sew blankets for homeless people, people in hospitals, and veterans.
