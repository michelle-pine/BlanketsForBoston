events

We host Blanket Building Events at local elementary schools throughout December. We want to get children and their families involved in community service during the winter, and blankets are a great way to bring comfort to those in need.

Our Blanket Building Events are large community gatherings hosted at public venues, the Elementary School. They are accessible and open to all. The more people who participate, the merrier.

At these events, people can make two blankets – one for themselves, and one to be donated to people in need. Fabric is provided. Participants can create our easy, no sew blankets, or they can sew something more complex as a challenge.

Blankets for Boston is currently a proposed winter activation activity for the Mayor's Office of New Urban Mechanics.
