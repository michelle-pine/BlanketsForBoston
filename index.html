Make your own
No Sew Blanket
Purchase 2 pieces of fleece of identical size, as large as you want (fleece is about $6.00 per yard at a your local fabric store).
Line up the two pieces of fabric. Trim the fabric so both pieces are even.
Cut 5 inch by 5 inch squares away from each corner of the fabrics (which are still lined up).
Cut the edges of the fabric pieces into 1-2 inch strips
Knot the aligning strips from each fabric together until you have a blanket!
